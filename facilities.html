Our Spaces

Every room is clean, well-maintained and ready for your event. All bookings include Wi-Fi, tea & coffee, and on-site support.

Main Hall

Seats up to 80 (theatre) or 50 (workshop). Projector, screen & sound system included. Ideal for conferences, talks & large gatherings.

Meeting & Seminar Rooms

Three rooms seating 8–20 each. Perfect for breakout sessions, board meetings, spiritual direction or small group work.

Chapel

A sacred, intimate space seating 30. Available for Mass, Adoration, or quiet contemplation. Available on request.

Catering & Kitchen

Commercial kitchen & dining area for catered lunches or self-catering. Tea, coffee & light refreshments arranged with notice.

Gardens & Grounds

Peaceful maintained gardens ideal for prayer walks, outdoor sessions or quiet breaks. Wheelchair accessible throughout.

Conference Room

A dedicated conference room seating up to 55, with presentation equipment, natural lighting, and a professional atmosphere suited to seminars, training days, and diocesan events.
