A peaceful, professional space in the heart of Charleville — available for your group, event, or organisation.

A Tranquil Setting for Any Occasion

The Shalom Retreat Centre offers a range of beautifully maintained, fully-equipped spaces suitable for days of reflection, workshops, training events, meetings, and more. Whether you are a faith community, a charity, or a corporate team seeking a calm and inspiring environment, we welcome you.

Main Hall

Our spacious main hall comfortably seats up to 80 people in a theatre style or 50 for workshops. Equipped with a projector, screen, and sound system, it is ideal for talks, conferences, and large gatherings.

Meeting & Seminar Rooms

Three smaller rooms seat 8–20 people each — perfect for breakout sessions, board meetings, small group prayer, or one-to-one spiritual direction. Natural light and a calm atmosphere throughout.

Chapel

A sacred and intimate space for prayer, reflection, and liturgy. The chapel seats 30 and provides a reverent atmosphere for Mass, Adoration, or quiet contemplation. Available on request.

Catering & Kitchen

A fully equipped commercial kitchen and dining area, with capacity for catered lunches, coffee mornings, or self-catering for day events. Tea, coffee, and light refreshments can be arranged with advance notice.

Gardens & Grounds

Peaceful, well-maintained gardens surrounding the centre — ideal for outdoor prayer walks, guided meditations, or simply a quiet break during your event. Wheelchair accessible pathways throughout.

Parking

Ample on-site parking for up to 40 vehicles, with additional street parking nearby. The centre is centrally located on Main Street, Charleville, and accessible by public transport.
